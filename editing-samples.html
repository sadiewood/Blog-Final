            Fight Club 2
        Summer 2023, Premier Pro

    "fight Club 2" (alternativly titles "Fight Club for Girls") is a silly little parody I made with my friend Keren during the summer of 2023. It is based on the premise of Fight Club being a more succesful and theraputic environment, one that would ultimatly be more succesful if it where run by women. It is a jab at people who glorify the unhealthy coping machanisms displayed in the origional Fight Club. 

            Gay People Can't Cook
        Fall 2023, Premier Pro

    "Gay People Can't Cook" was a project I had to do for cinematography, however it turned into a personal and cozy vlog that details my two best friends and I trying to cook dinner. It is silly and was very fun to film and edit together. This is cut down from roughly half an hour of footage. 

            The Artist's Letter Rack
        Fall 2023, Premier Pro

    "The Artist's Letter Rack" is a short film based on the painting of the same name by William Michael Harnett. It follows the descent into madness experienced by an artist who beleives they are seeing signs of a major conspiracy in their day to day life.
